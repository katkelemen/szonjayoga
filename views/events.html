2017, Spring retreat

5 days relax and unwind yoga retreat with farm experience and tours in the picturesque Liguria, Italy

SzonjaYoga and Campo di Luna agriturismo invites you to a unique yoga retreat held in the amazing Ligurian countryside in Italy. This place is a hidden gem on the top of the hills, close to the Ligurian sea where you can get away from the busy and stressful weekdays. During your stay in the farm you can relax and recharge in the peaceful tranquility, take part in meditation and yoga classes, enjoy delicious homemade Italian meals, ramble around the beautiful forest and olive fields, explore and wander around all the beauty this region can offer.

Programme

Relax and unwind your mind, body and soul. Quiet morning time and meditation followed by a yoga class on the top of the hill with a breath-taking view. Classes are focusing on stress relief, (re)connecting with ourselves. Classes are suitable for people with a wide range of yoga experience, including beginners. Optional afternoon yoga class is also available.

During your free time you can also participate in a wide range of affordable activities such as visit many of the amazing places around, just to name a few: Pisa, Cinque Terre, Golfo dei Poeti, Carrara Marble Caves. You can take part in Italian cooking classes, take gardening tips and advices or just stay in the farm and enjoy the view and the tranquillity.

About the accomodation

Throughout your stay, SzonjaYoga guests will be hosted at the Campo di Luna agriturismo The huge family house is built in a couple of years ago on a hill side, using natural materials, colours, beautifully renewed old wooden furniture. You will be surrounded by a huge olive grow, forests, hills and with the view of the Ligurian sea. Here you will have the opportunity to experience the beauty of nature, the quietness and tranquility.

The house follows the line of simplicity but it is welcoming and cozy. The kitchen is an important part of the Italian family’s daily life; that is why the kitchen and living room are opened together, creating a huge space. They have a wall to wall glass door which opens to the terrace and looking at the sea. The view is just spectacular! Can you imagine sitting in the terrace and watching the amazing sunset whilst eating the delicious homemade dinner?

The rooms are nice and cool during the hot summer days. They are not over furnished. The owners used natural materials and colours to make the rooms nice and cozy. There are rooms for 2 or 3 people. The beds are new and comfortable, so you can be assured that you will have a pleasant night's sleep during your stay. Every room has a bathroom with all the necessary facilities.

The farm intends to be as self-sufficient as it is possible. They have chosen to use renewable energy resources, what nature can give us. On the roof there are many solar panels that use the sun's rays to generate electricity or heating. Everyone in the farm drinks fresh water from a pure mountain spring. For the household water they built in huge tanks under the ground, collecting water from wood spring.

They also produce many kind of vegetables and fruit, they also have an olive grow with over 400 olive trees. They are using their own seasonal products for cooking and making their own olive oil. You will have the chance to taste these delicious products. From the garden to the table!

In the garden and on the terrace you will find lots of comfortable chairs, sun loungers where you can rest, relax and recharge.

Food

The price of this retreat includes the dinner on the arrival day and four vegetarian breakfasts. Non- vegetarian meals are also available but the staff must be notified in advance.

The farm tries to be as self-sufficient as possible while creating nutritious and delectable meals for its guests. Ingredients such as vegetables, fruit, olive oil all come directly from the farm. Other products (e.g. eggs, bread) are bought from local farms, family owned small businesses. The fresh drinking water comes straight from a spring captured from a nearby mountain spring.

They often make handmade cakes and pies for guests to enjoy.

Liguria has many local specialties such as the well-known pesto sauce. Because of the proximity to the sea, seafood is one of the major content of many local dishes. It is absolutely worth a try!

Breakfast

Breakfast on the farm takes place from 9.30 am and it is a traditional Italian breakfast. It includes coffee, tea, orange juice, homemade jam, bread, milk and biscuits. Fruit and vegetables from the farm are often included in the breakfast.

Snacks and drinks

Snacks and drinks are also available throughout the day. You will find nuts, seeds on the table and also a wide variety of fruits are available straight from the garden. There will be fresh spring water, kettle and different kind of teas if you feel thirsty.

Excluded: Alcoholic beverages, lunch, dinner

Location

The farm is located close to the small village of Falcinello in the hills of Sarzana 300 meters above sea level, at the board of south Liguria and Tuscany regions. The area around the farm is one of the most beautiful places in Italy, rich in history, culture and beauty of nature. It is a perfect mixture of hills, marble mountains, hiking trails, rivers, medieval castles, churches and one of the most beautiful beaches with small picturesque villages.

The farm itself is a large property where guests can stroll around, relax in the shade of olive trees, hike around the trails in the beautiful forest or just sit on a sun lounger on the terrace and wonder the view. It is the perfect place to get away from the busy and rushing world and reconnect with yourself, recharge and be lost in the beauty of nature.

Things to do (optionals)

Italian cooking class: Learn how to make famous (local) Italian dishes from fresh ingredients from farms or local products. Price includes the dinner/ lunch.

Walking tour, hiking: a nearly 3 hour long guided hiking tour on the board of the Ligurian and Tuscan hills.

City tours: Pisa, Sarzana and La Spezia, Golfo dei Poeti, Cinque Terre

Exploring the Carrara Marble Mountains: guided tour with a local geologist. These marbles were used for many of world most famous attraction’s such as Pantheon, Rome or Marble Arch, London

Castles around Falcinello: explore south Liguria’s hidden castles and beauties.

Exploring Palmaria Island: you will have the opportunity to visit one of the three beautiful nearby islands, the Palmaria Island. The island is located in the door of Golfo dei Poeti and in front of the city of Portovenere. You have the chance to roam around or just enjoy the unbelievably blue sea and the warm sun with the breath-taking view of the golf.

How to get there

By flight: Please book your flight to arrive at Pisa International Airport (PSA), Pisa, Peretola Airport (FLR), Florence or Genoa Airport (GOA), Genoa

By train: You can get a direct train from Pisa or Firenze to Sarzana. From Firenze it takes about 2 hours, from Pisa it takes about 50- 55 minutes.

From Genoa to Sarzana you need to change at La Spezia Centrale train station. The train takes between one and half to two hours, depending on the train you decide to take.
