Szonja is a London based ambitious Vinyasa Flow Yoga Teacher. She graduated as a Conductor (Conductive Education) in Pető Institute (Pető András College) in Budapest, Hungary. In 2015 she travelled to Spain to complete her 200hr Yoga Teacher Training in Vinyasa Flow and Hatha Yoga with Frog Lotus International. During her course she gained knowledge of pregnancy yoga and restorative yoga as well. Recently she has also become a certified Special Yoga for Special Children teacher. She had the honour to learn from Jo Manuel who is the Lead Practitioner and Founder of Special Yoga Foundation. Szonja has had a keen interest in sports since her childhood, she was a competitive swimmer and runner. Currently she enjoys running and yoga on a daily basis. She started yoga 5 years ago in order to release stress, sleep better and keep herself fit. She loves motivating others to succeed and intents to create a safe environment for her students in order to support inward focus and observation of the body, mind and spirit.
